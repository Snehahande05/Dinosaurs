Stage 6

Ice Age Emergence

A world transformed by glaciers, survival, and climatic extremes.

Overview

The Ice Age Emergence signifies the dramatic shift in Earth's climate that followed the dinosaur extinction. Gigantic glaciers covered vast landscapes, and megafauna like mammoths and saber-toothed cats ruled the frozen world. This era forged evolutionary resilience in the species that would one day shape our modern world.
