Volcanic Eruptions

Cataclysmic eruptions reshaped the Earth and disrupted life forever.

Overview

Massive volcanic eruptions released tremendous amounts of lava, ash, and gases into the atmosphere. This led to climate changes, acid rain, and toxic air, creating a hostile environment for many species, including dinosaurs.
