Age of Giants

The majestic reign of the colossal beings that once roamed Earth.

Overview

The Age of Giants marks a primeval era when Earth's ecosystems were dominated by enormous dinosaurs. From the towering Brachiosaurus to the ferocious Tyrannosaurus Rex, this stage witnessed the rise of reptilian titans. Their existence shaped prehistoric landscapes, and their fossils whisper tales of power, survival, and extinction.
