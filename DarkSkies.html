Stage 4

Dark Skies

The skies turned black. A world without sunlight. A silence where life once thrived.

What Happened?

After the asteroid impact, Earth's skies were choked with ash and debris. The sun was hidden for months, maybe years. Without light, photosynthesis stopped. Temperatures dropped. Ecosystems collapsed.

The Fallout

  • Global Winter: A sudden drop in temperature triggered mass deaths.
  • Plant Collapse: Without sunlight, plant life perished first.
  • Food Chain Disruption: Herbivores starved, followed by carnivores.

Echoes of Silence

A once-vibrant planet became a quiet, grey world. But out of that darkness, something new would one day rise.
